Team Dogez

A page about Team Dogez

The reason why I made this page is because I'm leader of Team Dogez. Team Dogez is a MMGG (Minecraft Mini-Game Group). And I have always loved programming, so I decided to use it for some purpose, and made Team Dogez. It's members are: Sparky01, RedCrystalSword, DogezMC_YT, Dangerous_DJ_YT, DarthChimera, HaileyLuvsHorses, loganwii2003, Vampire94937



Some of the mini-games we have made are: Dogez-Pocalypse, Dogez Run, Spleef, Ultimate Snowball Fight, and Zombie Apocalypse. We have had most of these mini-games on a Minecraft realm called: Dogez Studios, though it doesn't exist anymore.



The reason why our team is named after one of its members, DogezMC_YT, is because we all thought that 'Dogez' sounded and just went with it.
