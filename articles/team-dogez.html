The reason why I made this page is because I'm leader of Team Dogez. Team Dogez is a MMGG (Minecraft Mini-Game Group). And I have always loved programming, so I decided to use it for some purpose, and made Team Dogez. It's members are: Sparky01, RedCrystalSword, DogezMC_YT, Dangerous_DJ_YT, DarthChimera, HaileyLuvsHorses, loganwii2003, Vampire94937. We've worked very hard throughout all of the time Team Dogez existed, and even before then some of us we're still together making mini-games Most of the people on Team Dogez are newer players, only having played for 2-3 years. Also most people never knew how commands worked for a long time yet, almost every single Team Dogez game is based on command blocks. So basically: We've embarked on many quests, learned a whole ton, played way too much Minecraft, and overall become a pretty good team. Some of the mini-games we have made are: Dogez-Pocalypse, Dogez Run, Spleef, Ultimate Snowball Fight, and Zombie Apocalypse. We have had most of these mini-games on a Minecraft realm called: Dogez Studios, though it doesn't exist anymore. The reason why our team is named after one of its members, DogezMC_YT, is because we all thought that 'Dogez' sounded good, and just went with it.
